ABOUT LIBRUMI

A community reading room reimagined online — the same shelves, now a click away.

Made by readers,
run for readers.

Librumi keeps a curated collection of physical titles and lets you reserve them online. When a copy is free, request it — an librarian approves the loan and holds the book at the desk.

No membership fees. No late fines that hurt — just a gentle nudge to bring your book back so the next reader can enjoy it.
